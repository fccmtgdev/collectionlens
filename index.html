Smart Highlighting

EDHREC & Scryfall matching. Cards are highlighted based on your inventory. See green outlines for owned, orange for proxies, and striped patterns for copies you own but already in decks. Hover over highlighted cards to see a detailed breakdown of which of your decks currently contain those copies.

Collection & Decks

Import your collection via CSV from Moxfield or Deckbox. Sync your Moxfield decks automatically with one click, categorizing them dynamically.

Collection Import
Deck Management
📊

Detailed Tooltips & Badges

On sites like MTGTop8, MTGDecks, and MTGGoldfish, hover over missing cards to see an inventory badge: Total Owned, Available, and Needed for the current list. Never guess if your copy is tied up in another deck again.

💾

Local & Secure Backup

All data is stored locally. You can easily export and import your database (collection + decks + settings) via a `.json` file to move between devices seamlessly.

⚙️

Customizable Settings

Select "Count Decks as Collection" to use decklists to calculate what you own, pick custom colors for highlights, and toggle individual site support on or off as needed.
